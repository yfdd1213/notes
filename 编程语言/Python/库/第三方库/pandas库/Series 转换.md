- [[Series.astype()]]  数据类型转换
```python
Series.convert_dtypes([infer_objects, ...])
	# 自动转换最佳数据类型
Series.copy(deep=True)
	# 拷贝

Series.bool()

Series.to_numpy([dtype, copy, na_value])
```
